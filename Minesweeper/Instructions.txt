MINESWEEPER - HOW TO PLAY

Choose a difficulty to start the game.

The board is a grid of hidden cells (#).
Your goal is to reveal all safe cells without hitting a mine.

HOW TO PLAY:
- Enter a move as: row column (example: 2 3)
- This reveals the selected cell

WHAT HAPPENS:
- If you hit a mine, you lose
- Numbers show how many mines are nearby
- Empty cells (0) reveal surrounding areas

COMMANDS:
u = undo
r = redo
q = quit

WIN:
In order to win you must reveal all non-mine cells. Good Luck!
